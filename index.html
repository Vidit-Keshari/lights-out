Lights Out - Game Rules

  • The game board consists of a 3x3 grid of cells.
  • Your goal is to turn off all the cells to win.
  • Green cells are ON, and blue cells are OFF. So, you have to turn all cells blue
  • Turning all cells ON results in a loss.
  • Click on a cell to toggle its state.
  • Clicking a cell also toggles its adjacent neighbors (above, below, left, and right).
  • Plan your moves strategically to turn off all the cells!
  • Game resets automatically after 2 seconds if a win or lose is detected.
  • Clicks are disables on win or lose.
  • The game save your name by default. For more saving features see below.

Notes

  • If you do not enter your name, your name will be set to guest automatically in the system. This does not rename you globally in the 'Old Series'. Your name will NOT be saved in the system.
  • Saving the game and clearing the cache may delete the saved progress. For safety purposes, we recommend to keep a copy of the encoded binary code. The save button might not be liable in some cases.
  • Your player name is connected 'Vidit: Old Series: All games' player name. Renaming yourself in one game of 'Old Series' renames you in the whole 'Old Series'.
